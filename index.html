    F.A.Q

    • Why do people go to medical spas?

      Most people visit medical spas to receive treatments that could improve their appearance. Services such as exfoliation could help improve your skin's radiance and glow. Visiting a medical spa might help you age gracefully. Treatments like Botox could reduce the appearance of wrinkles on your face.

    • What do you wear and bring to a medical spa?

      Generally, though, you’ll want to bring something light, comfortable and easy to change out of and store.
      Flip-flops or open-toed sandals work well for shoes. Because you’ll be wearing a robe for most treatments, underwear that feels comfortable is also recommended.
      Jewelry is one clothing item that’s better to avoid or keep it at home when planning a spa day.

    • What is the difference between a health spa and a medical spa?

      Unlike day spas that focus on improving your outer appearance, medical spas provide medical procedures that are designed to not only improve the appearance but also repair your skin, hair, and even metabolic processes.

    • Do I need to make an appointment for a procedure at Medi-Mani?

      Yes

    • Why are med spas growing in popularity?

      Patients flock to med spas to receive a different type of care than that which doctors offer in their traditional practices. USA Today reports that, in 2013, med spas represented nearly $2 billion in revenue.
