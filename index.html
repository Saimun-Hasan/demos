Chief Business Developer

One Chief Business Development Officer is in place, leading five Regional BD Sales/Marketing personnel. These are often salon owners that made an L1 or EB5 (1.2M) investment!!!

  • Increase your revenue by investing as an L1 or EB5 visa holder.
  • No size limitation of company
  • After one year, you can apply for a Green Card under the Multinational Executive/Manager Category through the same Employer/business qualifying relationship, skipping crucial phases in the Green Card process.

Regional Chief

Regional Chief: Each Regional BD Sales is accountable for ten states.

  • Delegate your duties to regional executives to oversee the many firms across multiple states.
  • Improve your efficiency and production.
  • Regional Leaders will immediately report to you, keeping you up to date on what is going on in your branches.

State Leaders

Each state is led by a State BD Sales Head who owns the firm 100% and is supported by 5 area leaders.

  • The sales director owns and leads the program in each state (Clinician, Nail Owner, etc.).
  • Regional Leaders will be supported by State Heads who will monitor the branches in person to ensure that nothing falls through the cracks.
  • This will assist regional leaders in keeping track of and managing the process.
